Description

At VEEARR Polymarbles, we strive to provide high quality interior and exterior decoration products at affordable prices. The moto being "High Quality ≠ High Cost" ever since our launch in 2018. Increasing our product range since our launch, we now stand at 25+ product ranges, ranging from wall cladings, wall tiles to exterior grade veneer sheets. The one-stop shop for all your home decoration need.

Starting at 35 ₹ / sqft you can beautify your homes with VR Foam tiles. Multilayered foam based tiles for your wall and ceiling decorations.Peel and stick based method for efficient and easy installation.

Some of our unique propositions being PVC panels, which provide a light-weight, low cost alternative to paints, and marble based wall tiles. With over 50+ designs, ranging from matte to textured, providing a much better option than any other alternative at affordable pricing.
